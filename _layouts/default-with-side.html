About Me

Land, air and sea. Vehicles seem to be everywhere and anywhere. We seem to take our surroundings for granted but never thoroughly thought how such machines came to be and how they benefit society so much. I wondered how these vehicles ever worked in the first place. As a person whose interests lie in this field, I'd like more people to know about these machines and how useful they are.

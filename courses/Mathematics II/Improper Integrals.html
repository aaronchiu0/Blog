Mathematics II

Improper Integrals

There are two such types of Improper Integrals, Infinite and Discontinuous.

Infinite Integrals

$$\begin{aligned} \int_a^\infty f(x) \ dx &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \int_a^t f(x) \ dx & \text{Where $a$ is a constant} \\ \int_{-\infty}^b f(x) \ dx &= \lim_{t\to-\infty} \int_t^b f(x) \ dx & \text{Where $b$ is a constant} \end{aligned}$$ If the limit exists, the integrals are called convergent. If not, they are divergent.
